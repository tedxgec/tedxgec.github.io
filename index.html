TEDxGEC Salon: Emancipation of Education

The first TEDxGECSalon event was held on 21st April 2017 and was themed ‘Emancipation of Education’. A Salon event is similar to a standard event but with the inclusion of group discussions. The TEDx team took the decision to feature an equal number of teachers and students to engage in the discussion about the academics, campus, events, and prevailing culture in GEC.
